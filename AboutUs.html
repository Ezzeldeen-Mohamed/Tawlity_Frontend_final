About Us

What Tawlity Does

Tawlity is a cutting-edge platform designed to streamline the restaurant reservation process for customers and restaurant owners alike. The website provides an intuitive and user-friendly interface where customers can discover restaurants, view available tables, make reservations, and even order foodâ€”all in one place. On the other hand, restaurant owners can manage their tables, update menu items, handle reservations, and engage with customers effortlessly.

Our Purpose

The primary goal of Tawlity is to enhance the dining experience for customers and simplify restaurant management for owners. We aim to solve common issues such as overcrowding, unavailable reservations, and inefficient communication between customers and restaurants. Tawlity ensures that both sides have a smooth and efficient experience with the help of technology, allowing for better decision-making and a more organized approach to dining and reservations.

Key Features

  • Restaurant Listings: Discover nearby restaurants with available tables and ratings from other users.
  • Real-Time Reservation: View available slots and instantly book your table at your desired time.
  • Order Food Online: Place your order from the restaurant's menu before or after your reservation.
  • Table Management: Restaurant owners can easily manage table availability and optimize their seating arrangements.
  • User Profiles: Create and manage personal profiles for customers and restaurant admins for a tailored experience.
  • Payment Integration: Pay for your reservations or food orders securely via multiple payment methods like credit cards, PayPal, and mobile payments.
